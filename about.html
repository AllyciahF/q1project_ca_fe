Here we will show you the bar graph of the qualitative datas and for the quantitative the histograms will be shown. The survey itself was made brief by the people gathering data for the survey to be easily answerable, and they got 700+ responses in a day due to this. The dataset is considered to be a population as the data collectors collected as many responses as possible.
